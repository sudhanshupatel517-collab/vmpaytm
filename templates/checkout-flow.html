Checkout Flow

🛍 How It Works

Our secure and automated checkout process is simple:

  1. Browse & Select: Pick your desired products from the grid and add them to your cart.
  2. Review Cart: Click 'View Cart' to review quantities and total amount.
  3. Pay Securely: Click 'Proceed to Pay' to launch the Razorpay gateway. Use UPI, Card, or Netbanking.
  4. Verification: The system instantly verifies your payment status.
  5. Dispense: Upon success, the machine motors activate and drop your items.

⚠ Need Help?

In case of any payment failures or if the product gets stuck and is not dispensed, please check our Refund Policy or Contact Support.
