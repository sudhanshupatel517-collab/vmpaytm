Payment Checkout Flow

  1. User selects a product from the website or vending interface
  2. The system displays the payment option (QR / UPI / gateway)
  3. User completes the payment using their preferred method
  4. Payment is verified through the payment gateway
  5. Upon successful payment, the vending machine dispenses the selected product

In case of payment failure or product not being dispensed, users can contact support for assistance or refund.
